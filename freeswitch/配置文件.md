## 配置 FreeSwitch 作为软电话时候的电话号码

conf/dialplan/default/portaudio.xml

```
<include>
    <extension name="call me">
        <condition field="destination_number" expression="^(1111)$">
            <action application="bridge" data="portaudio" />
        </condition>
    </extension>
</include>
```
